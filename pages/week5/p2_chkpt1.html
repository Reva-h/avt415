Hahn & Fiedel

Family-friendly luthier and string instrument store. Also offers music lessons and serves as a small community venue for local musicians. A warm, earth-toned feel that will reference roosters (Hahn & Fiedel is German for Rooster and Fiddle)

Zest Tropical Fruits

Delivery service that brings fresh unique fruits from around the world to customers' doorsteps. A tropical, funky vibe.

Beans 'n Bytes

Silicon Valley based coffeeshop that markets itself to tech employees looking for a quick pick-me-up during the work day. Combines techy and coffeeshop aesthetic.
